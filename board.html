President

  • Professional Background

    Phil is a Financial Advisor with Your Bridge Wealth Management in Ashtabula, and previously worked as an Equity Research Analyst at Longbow Research in Indepdence, Ohio. He is a graduate of Geneva High School, Mount Union College (Undergraduate), and Case Western Reserveâ€™s Weatherhead School (M.B.A.)

  • Soccer Experience/Volunteerism

    Phil is active in the community, also serving on the Board of the Geneva Area Chamber of Commerce, and formerly as a Board Member for the Ashtabula Arts Center and the Northeast Ohio Regional Airport. Phil played soccer between the ages of 9 and 18 in Geneva rec leagues and for Geneva High School, and has pursued additional coaching education including the U.S. Soccer Grassroots licenses and the U.S. Soccer D Coach License. He has coached rec or travel soccer for the past eight years.

  • Personal

    Phil and his wife Allison are both Ashtabula County natives, and live in Geneva with their three children, Graham, Sawyer, and Julia, all of whom play soccer.
