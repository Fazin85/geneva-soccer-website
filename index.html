Youth Soccer Programs

Recreational Soccer - Spring 2025 Registration Now Open!

Our recreational program is designed for players of all skill levels looking to learn the game in a fun, supportive environment. Games are played locally in Geneva with an emphasis on skill development and enjoying the sport.

Program Highlights:

  • Ages 4-14 welcome
  • Weekly practices and weekend games
  • Equal playing time for all participants
  • Focus on fundamental skills and teamwork
  • All games played in Geneva

We are always in need of Recreational Soccer Coaches. If you are interested in coaching a team, please sign up when registering your child.

Register for Recreational Soccer

Travel Soccer - Spring 2025 Registration Now Open!

Our travel program offers a more competitive experience for players looking to advance their skills. Teams compete against other communities throughout the region, with professionally developed training sessions.

Program Highlights:

  • U8-U14 age divisions
  • Twice weekly practices with certified coaches
  • Weekend games throughout the region
  • Focus on advanced skills and tactical development
  • Tryouts may be required depending on registration numbers

Travel teams require a higher level of commitment from both players and parents, including possible travel to away games up to 1 hour from Geneva.
