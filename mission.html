Geneva Soccer League Mission Statement

Our mission is to promote, teach, and enjoy the game of soccer. Through soccer, young athletes not only learn individual skills, they learn the importance of teamwork and accountability.

Soccer demands physical strength and endurance while also requiring teamwork, calculation, and creativity. We believe that developing these skills empowers our young athletes on and off the field.

Geneva Soccer is a nonprofit organization that is dedicated to creating a safe, fun, and positive soccer culture.

Our Core Values

Fun

We know there are lots of activities competing for kid's time, and that they will stick with what is most enjoyable. We strive to make soccer fun at both the rec and travel levels.

Development

Just as more dedicated rec players can progress to travel, there are additional pathways to more competitive soccer for players who need that challenge. We are also happy to help first time players develop a love for the game.
