U13 Soccer Rules & Guidelines

Rules for players ages 10-13

Upper Division Rules & Guidelines

Play Format

7v7 – Six (6) field players and one (1) Goalkeeper. Size modification is encouraged.

Game Time

Two (2) 30-minute halves – total of 60 minutes playing time. Halftime is 5 minutes.

Substitutions: Throw-ins, goal kicks, after scoring, injuries & referee permission.

Coaching

Coaches are not allowed on the field. No one should be behind the goals.

Positions

All players should be rotated to all positions. Players can play goalkeeper one (1) half per game.

Playing Time

Each player should play a minimum half of every game. Recreation soccer does not require attendance at practice to play in the game.

Goal Scoring

Players can score three (3) goals. After three (3) goals, please move them to a defensive position or goalie.

Goal Kicks

Goal kicks are taken from the goal box. Opposing players must be outside of the Penalty Area.

Corner Kicks

Corner kicks are taken at the corner of the field – offense allowed one.

Throw Ins

Feet must be outside or on the sideline (side-line). Players are allowed one (1) re-throw. Coach or referee should help for a proper throw-in.

Fouls & Kicks

Indirect kicks only. No Penalty Kicks – Indirect kick is taken at the top of the box.

Slide Tackling

Slide tackling is PROHIBITED. Slide tackling and slide kicking are different.

Cherry Picking

An offensive player should not be instructed to stand in front of the goal and stay there. Attacking players should be actively involved in play.

Scores

Formal scores are not kept, but if a team falls behind by three (3) goals, that team can add a player to the field. If the team scores and is down by two (2) goals, that team should remove the added player.

A second player may be added if the team is behind by six (6) goals, or the winning team can remove a player.

Shin Guards

Shin guards are MANDATORY – they should be worn under the socks. A player will NOT be allowed to play without shin guards.

Clothing

The game shirt should be the outermost garment.

Soft hats are permitted for warmth (NO baseball-style or brimmed caps allowed). Cleats are recommended.

Heading

ABSOLUTELY NO HEADING AT GAMES OR PRACTICE.

NOTE

There are times when modifications to the rules/guidelines are needed for the best interest of the players and the game.

A Staff or Board Member has the authority to modify these rules/guidelines. Their decision shall be final.
