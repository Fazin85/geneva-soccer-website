U5 Soccer Rules & Guidelines

Rules for players ages 4-5

General Rules

Team Format

Teams will play with 3 players on the field and a goalie. Total on field (4).

Game Time

Games will consist of (6) 5-minute periods. Time between periods will be no more than 2 minutes to allow substitutions. Halftime will be between periods 3 and 4 and will be 5 minutes.

Coaching

One or two coaches are allowed on the field to help the players, but should stay out of the way of play. Coaches on field should help players spread out and not bunch together.

Player Rotation

All players should be rotated to each position and should play a minimum of half of every game.

Goal Scoring

No player is allowed to score more than 3 goals. If player scores 3 goals, please move them to a defensive position or goalie.

Shin Guards

SHIN GUARDS MUST BE WORN AT ALL TIMES. No one is allowed to play without them. Hats are permitted for warmth but NO baseball hats.

Goal Scoring Rules

Goals can only be scored from the offensive side of the field. If a goal is kicked from the defensive side of the field, the goal does not count, and a goal kick will be awarded to the other team.

Semi-Circle Rules

No one is allowed in the semi-circle in front of the goal except for the goalie. Only the goalie may use his or her hands to pick up the ball or kick the ball to a teammate. NO players may kick the ball once it is inside the circle (safety for U6 goalie).

Goal Kicks

Goal kicks are taken from anywhere on the semi-circle. During goal kicks all defensive players must stand behind the half line until offensive team touches the ball.

Corner Kicks

Corner kicks are taken from the corners of the field by the offensive team.

Throw-Ins

On an illegal throw-in, players will be allowed to re-throw. Coach or referee should help players correct the mistake. Players can re-throw 2 times. Both feet need to stay on the ground with ball in both hands as an overhead throw above the head going forward to a teammate.

Slide Tackling

Slide tackling is prohibited.

Cherry Picking

"Cherry picking", where an offensive player is positioned in front of the goal and instructed to stay there, is NOT PERMITTED.

Score Management

If a team falls behind by 3 goals, they can add a player to the field. If a goal is scored and the team is behind by 2 goals, the player should be removed. A second player may be added if the team is behind by 6 goals or the winning team can remove a player.

Heading

NO HEADING ALLOWED. NOT IN GAMES OR IN PRACTICE

NOTE

There are times when modifications to the rules/guidelines are needed for the best interest of the players and the game. A Staff or Board Member has the authority to modify these rules/guidelines. Their decision shall be final.
