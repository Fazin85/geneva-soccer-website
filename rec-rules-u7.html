U7 Soccer Rules & Guidelines

Rules for players ages 6-7

Play Format

6v6 - Five (5) field players and one (1) Goalkeeper. Size modification is allowed.

Game Time

Four (4) ten-minute periods – total of 40 minute playing time. There is a two (2) minute break between periods. Halftime is five (5) minutes.

Coaching

One coach allowed on the field & one coach allowed by their own goal. Coaches should stay out of the area of play.

Positions

All players should be rotated to all positions. Players can play goalkeeper one (1) quarter per game.

Playing Time

Each player should play a minimum half of every game. Recreation soccer does not require attendance at practice to play in the game.

Goal Scoring

Players can score three (3) goals. After three (3) goals please move them to a defensive position or goalie.

Goal Kicks

Goal kicks are taken from inside the goal box. Opposing players must be at least 10 feet away from the person taking the goal kick & outside the goal box.

Corner Kicks

Corner kicks are taken at the corner of the field – offensive team only.

Throw Ins

Feet must be outside or on the touchline (side-line). Players are allowed two re-throws. Coach or referee should help for a proper throw-in.

Slide Tackling

Slide tackling is PROHIBITED. Slide tackling and slide kicking are different.

Cherry Picking

An offensive player should not be instructed to stand in front of the goal and stay here. Attacking players should be actively involved in play.

Scores

Formal scores are not kept, but if a teams falls behind by three (3) goals that team can add a player to the field. If the team scores and is down by two (2) goals that team should remove the added player.

Note: A second player may be added if the team is behind by six (6) goals or the winning team can remove a player.

Shin Guards

Shin guards are MANDATORY – they should be worn under the socks. A player will NOT be allowed to play without shin guards.

Clothing

The game shirt should be the outermost garment. Soft hats are permitted for warmth (NO baseball style or brimmed caps allowed). Cleats are recommended.

Heading

NO HEADING IS ALLOWED DURING GAMES OR PRACTICE.

Build Out Line

All defensive players must remain behind the Half line until the ball is touched by opposing team during Goal Kicks or when awarded a Free Kick.

NOTE

There are times when modifications to the rules/guidelines are needed for the best interest of the players and the game. A Staff or Board Member has the authority to modify these rules/guidelines. Their decision shall be final.
