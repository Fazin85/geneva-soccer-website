Who Can Play

We accept registrations for players ages 4 to 13, although divisions vary by season.

Rec soccer is appropriate for beginner players and anyone trying soccer for the first time.

What to Expect

Practice Sessions

Coaches select practice days with most teames practicing 2x per week for an hour to an hour and a half.
